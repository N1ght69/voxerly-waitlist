This Cookie Policy explains how Voxerly ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on voxerly.xyz (the "Site"). By using the Site, you consent to the use of cookies and local storage as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Similar technologies include local storage, which stores data directly in your browser without an expiry date.

2. How We Use Cookies and Local Storage

We use the following technologies on the Site:

Essential Local Storage

We use your browser's local storage to remember functional information such as whether you have already signed up to our waitlist, and whether you have acknowledged our cookie banner. This storage is strictly necessary for the Site to function correctly and cannot be disabled.

Data stored: waitlist signup status, cookie consent preference, rate limiting timestamps.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Site. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information such as how many people visit the Site, which pages they visit, how long they stay, and where they come from. This data is aggregated and anonymous — we cannot identify individual visitors from it.

Google Analytics cookies set: _ga, _ga_*, _gid

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by visiting: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

For more information on how Google uses your data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

3. Third-Party Cookies

We do not serve advertising cookies or allow third-party advertisers to place cookies on the Site. We do not sell or share cookie data with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Your Choices

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings — Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Site.
  • Google Analytics opt-out — Install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Local storage — You can clear local storage data at any time through your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage → Clear)
